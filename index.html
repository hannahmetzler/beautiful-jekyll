I am a young scientist with a background in psychology and social and affective neuroscience, and an enthusiast advocate of open science and effective altruism. As a postdoc in Computational Social Science at the Complexity Science Hub and Medical University of Vienna, I currently work on:

  • Collective emotions in response to the COVID-19 pandemic using social media analysis
  • Characteristics of media reports about suicide that could contribute to prevention
  • Emotional Misinformation: how do emotions influence the spreading of misinformation?
